Heros Of Lowhelm

About Us

What Is HOL?


Heroes of Lowhelm is a turn based RPG build within the Zilliqa blockchain. LOH will challenge players to overcome amazing battles with stratagy whilst submerging the users within a surreal environment.

The 2D aspect of HOL will create nostalgia within the community and will allow users to relive their past gaming experiences like never before. With this in mind, we have set out to deliver a 2D game that offers unparalleled levels of animation quality to tap into that nostalgia.

The stories and quests within HOL will set this game apart from many other 2D based games on the market, when including the power of the Zilliqa blockchain and NFTs this sets HOL onto another level.

Early on, we have focused on how the events took place that left Lowhelm in dust, engaging with the user and explaining the story as if they were there.

The events of the game unravel as the users grinds through missions and objections to achieve the unlikely goal of defeating the invaders and regenerating their past.

HOL uses the power of NFTs and the Zilliqa blockchain to reward users and engage them along the epic story line. Users can unlock chests receive power ups and even enhance weapons with so much more coming to this amazing 2D experience.

Who Is Behind HOL?


HOL is designed by avid RPG game fans looking to seek the funding needed to develop their life long ambition of bringing a mainstream adoptable game to the Zilliqa blockchain.

HOL will connect the Zilliqa blockchain directly to the game, allowing users to purchase in-game items and NFTS.

Where Is The Game Now?


HOL is a working product, with a trailer in development of the game and several concept and working images being released very soon.

The funding received from the token sale will go directly on hiring a game studio to finalize and develop the game to a high professional standard.

The Game

The Beginning


Set in the year 1200 on distant planet in the Aura system, Lowhelm is at war. The planet has been targeted for harvesting by the Cryptic monsters, putting all their lives and worldly possessions into rebut. The king, Aldrich V has called upon the greatest champions from his kingdoms to help repel the monster hoards.

The cryptic monsters are thing of nightmares. They utilize the divine powers of earth, water, fire , light, and darkness, making them unstoppable to those without the knowledge.

Taking On The Challenge


Heroes who decide to take on the challenge of the Cryptic hoards will need to play with strategy and instincts by playing against the elements and leading Lowhelm to victory.



There is over 12 heroes awaiting your command, all having different skills and abilities which can be enhanced and powered up by the legendary elemental shards.

Where Does Zilliqa Fit In?


Zilliqa will play a vital roll in the Heroes of Lowhelm game. Players can purchase shards, weapons and so much more with the native HOL token, with all purchases from the HOL system being burnt.

The game itself is free to play, but users looking to enhance their experience can buy amazing perks on the HOL marketplace.

*Heroes of Lowhelm is a working product, with illustrations and trailers coming very soon. The token raise for HOL is to help fund the gaming studio to finish the game on the Zilliqa blockchain.
